Marketplace

OctaSpace operates a decentralized computing marketplace where users can offer hardware for rental in the form of nodes, with providers setting custom pricing based on hardware configuration. This decentralized approach creates a dynamic and competitive ecosystem, promoting the democratization of computing and encouraging competitive pricing.

OctaSpace supports and provides a wide range of hardware, including both CPUs and GPUs from major manufacturers. Nodes can range from server-grade multi-GPU configurations for heavy compute tasks to Raspberry Pi’s serving as VPN exit nodes.

Dedicated compute resources

When a user rents a node from the marketplace, they are allocated all of the node’s resources, including CPU, RAM, GPU(s), and storage.
